Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how our Instagram Automation Application collects, uses, and protects your information when you interact with our service.

1. Information We Collect

Our application accesses only the necessary data provided via Meta APIs to function effectively:

  • Message Content: We process incoming Direct Messages to identify specific keywords (e.g., "menu").
  • User IDs: Unique Instagram scoped IDs to send automated replies back to the user.
  • Comments: Public comments on our business profile to trigger automated private replies.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide instant automated responses based on user-initiated keywords.
  • To improve customer service efficiency and response times.
  • To deliver requested information (such as digital catalogs or price lists) directly to the user.

3. Data Sharing and Retention

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Data is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers after the automated interaction is completed, unless required for basic logging and analytics purposes.

4. Data Deletion and User Rights

Users can stop interacting with the bot at any time by simply stopping the conversation. If you wish to request the deletion of any interaction logs, please contact us via Instagram Direct message.

This application complies with the Meta Platform Terms and Developer Policies.
